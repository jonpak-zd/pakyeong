Brought up majoring in drums and minoring in guitar, Pak Yeong was exposed to electronic dance music during his early college years. In the year 2016, he began to pursue the art of Djing seriously and began working in Deadwood Entertainment, one of the leading entertainment industries in the local club scene here in KL. Pak Yeong was a resident DJ in Como KL for their Saturday nights [Grand Theft Saturdays Remixed] and is now currently playing in other different clubs around the city. He specialises in house and trap music but music genres included in his sets include RnB, hip-hop, future bass, big room and EDM.
